ABOUT US

Making connections fun again, professionally.

A platform for learning and collaboration

"Kosmikos" is the Greek word for Cosmos, the entire physical universe. We couldn't have discovered anything in the universe if not for mentor-student collaboration, and in this era of shorter attention spans and fast-moving lives, Kosmikos strives to connect mentors and students like never before.

Academic Networking

Build professional relationships with mentors, peers, and educators.

Real-Time Updates

Stay up to date with announcements, deadlines, and discussions.

About the creator

Divya is a 19-year-old Computer Science student studying in Women's Christian College, Chennai. The lack of interactive and intuitive social media platforms that are professional, yet fun, inspired her to create Kosmikos. The ingenuity and simplicity of Tumblr and MySpace drove her to make the website similar to something that you might've found on your home computer from the 2000s but better.
